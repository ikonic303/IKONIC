Storefront & Building Signage in Denver, Colorado

ikonic designs, fabricates, and installs storefront and building signage across the Denver metro — from clean vinyl lettering and window graphics to illuminated channel letters and monument signs. The sign on your building is the first thing a customer reads about you; we make it say the right thing, clearly, and on-brand.

What we make

Illuminated channel letters and lit signs, monument and pylon signs, dimensional letters and logos, vinyl lettering, banners, and full storefront window graphics and branding. If it puts your brand on your building, we build and hang it.

Window graphics & storefront branding

Turn plain glass into a storefront that sells — window graphics, frosted privacy vinyl, hours and service lists, and full storefront branding that matches your signage and your vehicles, so every touchpoint looks like the same company.

Installed on-site, done right

Building and storefront signage is installed on-site at your location — surveyed, mounted, and finished by our team. (Vehicle wraps are the exception: those are done at our Wheat Ridge shop by drop-off appointment.) We confirm the surface and mounting on-site so the sign goes up safely and stays up.

Design, permit, fabricate, install

We handle it end to end: design that matches your brand, the drawings your city's permit process needs, fabrication, and a clean install. Many exterior and illuminated signs must meet local sign code — we help you get it right the first time instead of redoing it.

Serving the Denver metro

Storefront and building signage for businesses in Wheat Ridge, Arvada, Lakewood, Golden, and across greater Denver. Need interior directional or ADA signage too? See our wayfinding & ADA signage.

Signage FAQ

What kinds of signage do you make?

Storefront and building signs, channel letters, monument and pylon signs, dimensional letters, vinyl lettering, window graphics and storefront branding, banners, and interior branding.

Do you install on-site?

Yes — building and storefront signage is installed on-site across the Denver metro. Only vehicle wraps are shop drop-off.

Do you handle permits?

Many exterior and illuminated signs need a city permit and must meet local sign code. We help identify what's required and prepare the drawings the permit needs.

How long does a sign take?

Vinyl and window graphics are fast; fabricated and illuminated signs take longer, plus any permit time. You get a firm timeline with your quote.
