Commercial Vehicle Wraps in Denver, Colorado

ikonic designs, prints, and installs commercial and fleet vehicle wraps for local service businesses across the Denver metro. 3M-certified materials, installed in our Wheat Ridge shop by drop-off appointment — so your truck becomes the cheapest, hardest-working advertising you own.

What we wrap

Full wraps, partial wraps, and color changes on vans, box trucks, trailers, pickups, and whole fleets. Whether you want your logo and phone number clean on the doors or a full designed skin that turns heads at every stoplight, we build it to match your brand and print it to last.

Full wrap vs. partial wrap

Coverage level is the single biggest driver of what a wrap costs and how it looks. A partial wrap (doors, tailgate, a rear panel) is the affordable entry point and still gets your brand in front of everyone who sees the vehicle. A full wrap skins the whole body for a true color change and the most designed surface. Many owners start with a partial and step up to a full wrap once the leads start coming in.

Why a wrap is the cheapest advertising you own

Unlike an ad you rent every month, a wrap is a one-time cost that keeps working every time the truck is on I-25, parked at a job site, or sitting in a customer's driveway. Spread across the years a quality wrap stays sharp, the cost per impression is lower than almost any paid channel a local business can buy. It is a marketing asset you own, not a bill you pay again next month.

Installed in-shop, done right

Vehicle wraps at ikonic are installed in our Wheat Ridge shop by drop-off appointment — not on-site. A controlled, dust-free, temperature-stable bay is the difference between a wrap that lays down perfectly and one that bubbles or lifts. We use 3M-certified film and installation so your wrap holds up to Colorado sun and winters for years. (On-site installation is available for building and storefront signage.)

Serving the Denver metro

We wrap vehicles for businesses in Wheat Ridge, Arvada, Lakewood, Golden, and across the greater Denver area. Drop the vehicle at the shop by appointment and pick it up transformed.

Commercial wrap FAQ

How much does a commercial vehicle wrap cost in Denver?

It depends mostly on coverage, the vehicle's size and shape, the film, and the design and install complexity. A simple partial on a compact van is at the low end; a full color-change wrap on a box truck is at the high end. Rather than guess with a number that's wrong for most trucks, we quote your exact vehicle after a quick free walkthrough.

On-site or at your shop?

Vehicles are wrapped at our Wheat Ridge shop by drop-off appointment. On-site work is only for building and storefront signage.

How long does a wrap last in Colorado?

A wrap in quality 3M-certified cast film, cared for normally, holds up for years of Colorado sun and winters. Film choice and install quality matter as much as the design.

How long will my truck be in the shop?

A typical commercial wrap is a multi-day job once the design is approved and printed. We give you a firm timeline with your quote so you can plan around the vehicle being down.
