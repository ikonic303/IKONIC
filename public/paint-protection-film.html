FAQ

PPF questions

How much does paint protection film cost in Denver?

Partial front starts around $899, full front around $1,799, and full-body wraps from $5,999 — final pricing depends on your vehicle and film line. Get an exact number from a free assessment.

What's the difference between partial and full front?

Partial covers the bumper, the leading edge of the hood, fender edges, and mirrors. Full front extends to the entire hood and fenders plus headlights and A-pillars — the complete highway rock-chip zone. Use the visualizer above to see the difference.

Does PPF really self-heal?

Yes — quality films have a self-healing topcoat; minor scratches and swirls disappear with heat from the sun or warm water.

Can I add ceramic coating over PPF?

Absolutely — ceramic on top of PPF adds gloss and makes the film easier to keep clean. Ask us about bundling.
